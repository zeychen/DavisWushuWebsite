Chinese Martial Arts Tournament
The Chinese Martial Arts Tournament (CMAT) is held by Cal Wushu every spring at Haas Pavilion. In the tournament, about 500 martial artists will compete in divisions such as Contemporary/Traditional Wushu, Taiji, and Pushhands. In contemporary/traditional wushu, there are categories such as Chang Quan (long fist), Nan Quan(southern fist), and weapons such as broadsword, long sword, spear, pu dao, rope dart, and chain whip.
More details to come.
Davis Dance Revolution
Davis Dance Revolution (DDR) is an annual dance competition held at the end of the campus wide Picnic Day event at UC Davis. Various dance clubs at Davis enter to compete at a chance to win $500 for their respective clubs. Come check us out as we defend our champion title in the Traditional Category in the upcoming DDR for 2017.
